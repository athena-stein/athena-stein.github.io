[Establishing naturalistic brain stimulation targeting aperiodic EEG features: Transcranial Endogenous Current Stimulation (tECS)](/files/14_stein_2026_tecs.pdf) My first-author Brain Stimulation paper introducing tECS, a biomimetic stimulation framework in which waveforms are derived from an individual’s EEG, parametrically edited, and re-delivered as neuromodulation. ### 2.
