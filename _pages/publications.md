<h2>Featured publications</h2>

<ul>
  <li>
    <p>
      <strong>Stein A</strong>, Floegel E, Zhang M, Subramaniyan A, Sharma S, Feldman Z, Evers M, Riddle J, Meltzer-Brody S, Frohlich F. (2026).
      <a href="/files/14_stein_2026_tecs.pdf"><em>Establishing naturalistic brain stimulation targeting aperiodic EEG features: Transcranial Endogenous Current Stimulation (tECS).</em></a>
      <em>Brain Stimulation: Basic, Translational, and Clinical Research in Neuromodulation.</em>
    </p>
    <p>
      First-author Brain Stimulation paper introducing tECS, a biomimetic stimulation framework in which waveforms are derived from an individual’s EEG, parametrically edited, and re-delivered as neuromodulation.
    </p>
  </li>

  <li>
    <p>
      <strong>Stein A*</strong>, Schwippel TU*, Pupillo FM, LaGarde HC, Zhang M, Rubinow DR, Frohlich F. (2026).
      <a href="/files/13_stein_2026_glad2.pdf"><em>Daily EEG reveals stage-specific alpha power and functional connectivity modulation across five days of tACS in major depressive disorder.</em></a>
      <em>medRxiv.</em>
    </p>
    <p>
      First-author preprint examining the daily trajectory of EEG target engagement across five days of 10 Hz tACS in major depressive disorder.
    </p>
  </li>

  <li>
    <p>
      <strong>Stein A</strong>, Riddle J, Caulfield KA, Dux PE, Friehs MA, Schroeder PA, Craven MP, Groom MJ, Iyer KK, Barlow KM. (2025).
      <a href="/files/10_stein_2025_athometdcs.pdf"><em>Safety, Feasibility, and Tolerability of Ten Days of At-Home, Remotely Supervised tDCS During Gamified Attention Training in Children with Acquired Brain Injury: An Open-Label, Dose-Controlled Pilot Trial.</em></a>
      <em>Brain Sciences.</em>
    </p>
    <p>
      First-author clinical trial demonstrating the safety and feasibility of remotely supervised at-home brain stimulation in pediatric acquired brain injury.
    </p>
  </li>
</ul>
